Memoji

Memojis are animated emojis that look like real people from Apple. Apple first released a feature called Animoji in 2017, these animated emojis were able to track your face to make it appear as if you were a talking emoji in iMessage or FaceTime. Then following this release, Apple released Memojis in 2018. In other words, Memojis are basically personalized Animojis that look like the owner of the Apple product where they’re being used.

What is a Memoji?

The goal of Memoji is to create an avatar that looks just like you. The Memoji builder in your Apple device lets you customize everything. Including skin tone, hair color, hairstyle, hat type, eye shape, and so much more to create a really realistic emoji. Momoji by Apple is a great feature to get users to form a stronger, more personal connection to their device. This means people will spend more time on their Apple products, which leads to increased profits for Apple—pretty smart!
